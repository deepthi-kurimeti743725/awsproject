🎬 About Movie Magic

Movie Magic is a smart ticket booking system designed to simplify your cinema experience. Whether you're a movie buff or a casual watcher, our platform makes booking seats a breeze.

Built with ❤️ by a passionate student exploring Flask, HTML, CSS, and Python to bring creativity into code. It includes:

  • 🎟️ Secure Login & Register
  • 📅 Real-Time Ticket Booking
  • 🧠 Password Recovery
  • 📩 Contact & Help Pages

This project is part of an academic goal and personal dream — to build beautiful, functional software and learn along the way.
